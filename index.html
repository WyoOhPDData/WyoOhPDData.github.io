These documents came from Wyoming mayor's court. They include a breakdown of citations given in the year 2019, and the monthly fees collected by the City. Most notably, the handwritten amount at the bottoms of each monthly reports labeled "To the city" goes to the Wyoming city general fund. The yearly total in 2019 for the offenses listed is $128,000 JUST to the city.
